Chhattisgarh

Empowering tribal and underprivileged communities in naxalite-affected areas

Home / Work Areas / Chhattisgarh

Actively working in naxalite-affected areas of Chhattisgarh, with a strong focus on the Gond community, to engage youth in constructive, skill-based pathways.

Dedicated to the upliftment and welfare of tribal and underprivileged communities, particularly the Gond group.

Our Initiatives

Programs & Impact

◆

Skill Development & Education Infrastructure

Operating the Tathagat Industrial Training Institute (ITI), providing industry-relevant vocational and skill development training for tribal and underprivileged youth. Ensuring free education up to Class 12 to remove financial barriers and promote continuity in learning.

Early Childhood & Foundational Learning

Established a Bal Vatika to Primary school on Kanker–Balod Road, strengthening early childhood education in underserved areas.

Youth Empowerment & Career Readiness

Providing career counselling and skill-based coaching for Gond Adivasi youth (18+), supporting their transition into dignified employment and sustainable entrepreneurship opportunities. A school from Pre-Primary to Class 12 is being established in Bhainsa Village to provide quality, inclusive education for children of the underprivileged Satnami community.

Women Empowerment & Social Inclusion

Promoting girl-child education through free education and reserved seats for girls from marginalised and backward communities, while also strengthening mothers' skills to support sustainable livelihoods.

Holistic Development & Sports

Providing physical and mental development training for children to build confidence, discipline, and resilience through sports and co-curricular activities.

Strategic Partnerships

Partnering with the Jaquar Foundation to enhance training quality, infrastructure support, and job placement opportunities for students.
