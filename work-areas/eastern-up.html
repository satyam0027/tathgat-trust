Our Initiatives

Programs & Impact

◆

Community Focus

Supports backward and underprivileged communities like Bawaria, Kol, Vanwasi (Musahar), Bhil, and Gond in Sonbhadra and Mirzapur through education and skill development.

Free & Inclusive Education

Provides free or fully supported education and training, reserving 50% seats for girls, differently-abled, widows' dependents, and tribal youth.

Empowerment & Rehabilitation

Offers vocational training and skills to marginalized groups, promoting social acceptance and dignity.

Higher Education & Leadership

Supports meritorious students to access reputed institutions and prepares youth for competitive exams and leadership roles.

Social Protection

Creates pathways to reduce social exclusion, criminalization, and extremist influences by fostering sustainable opportunities.
