Banaras Public School: mainstreaming marginalized communities through education

Home / Work Areas / Banaras – BPS

Banaras Public School (BPS) is a flagship initiative of Tathagat Trust in the remote areas of Varanasi district, dedicated to mainstreaming marginalized communities through education and skill development.

Excellence & Access

School Programs

◆

Academic Excellence & Accessibility

CBSE-affiliated, subsidized education up to Class XII. Financial aid and fee waivers for underprivileged students. 45% girls enrolled; 300+ girls receive free education.

Infrastructure & Development

Modern labs, smart classrooms, library, and sports facilities. Focus on intellectual, emotional, and physical growth.

Collaborations & Impact

Works with NGOs to support underprivileged children. Empowers marginalized youth through education and skills.
