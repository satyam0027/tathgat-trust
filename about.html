About Us

Tathagat Trust is dedicated to empowering youth through skill-based education and inclusive learning. The Trust provides quality technical and vocational training to underprivileged and marginalized communities, especially in Naxal-affected regions, helping students overcome socio-economic barriers and improve employability. The Trust supports and facilitates the dignified marriage of underprivileged girls every year.

Special focus is given to communities that were historically notified as criminal tribes during the British period, with the objective of decriminalization through education, skills, and dignity, and their integration into the mainstream of Bharat.

Maruti Suzuki India Limited supports Tathagat Trust in Uttar Pradesh under its CSR initiative, contributing to training quality, infrastructure, and industry-oriented skill development. The Jaguar Foundation is supporting similar initiatives of the Tathagat Trust in Chhattisgarh.

The Trust actively works with socially excluded and marginalized communities, including those from Naxal-affected regions; Bheel communities of Rajasthan; Kol and Vanvasi (Musahar) communities in Uttar Pradesh; the Bawaria community across Rajasthan, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh; and other historically marginalized groups.

Vision

To create a skilled, confident, and socially empowered generation by ensuring equal access to quality education, promoting girl child empowerment, and preparing youth to meet contemporary and global standards.

Through education and skill development, Tathagat Trust envisions transforming historically marginalized and conflict-affected communities into self-reliant, skilled contributors to the nation, fully integrated into the social and economic mainstream of Bharat.
