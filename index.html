About Tathagat Trust

◆

Tathagat Trust is dedicated to empowering youth through skill-based education and inclusive learning, providing quality technical and vocational training to underprivileged and marginalized communities, especially in Naxal-affected regions. The Trust also supports and facilitates the dignified marriage of underprivileged girls every year.

Our Work Areas

◆

Reaching the most marginalized communities across three states of India

Chhattisgarh

Operating ITI and schools in naxalite-affected areas with focus on Gond Adivasi youth empowerment and tribal community upliftment.

Learn more →

Eastern Uttar Pradesh

Supporting Bawaria, Kol, Vanwasi communities in Sonbhadra and Mirzapur with free, inclusive education and vocational training.

Banaras (BPS)

CBSE-affiliated Banaras Public School mainstreaming marginalized communities through quality education up to Class XII.
