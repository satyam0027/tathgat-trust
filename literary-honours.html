Tathagat Trust Literary Honours

Celebrating Literature | Preserving Languages | Honouring Excellence

Home / Literary Honours

Tathagat Trust is also a national literary institution committed to promoting Indian languages and recognising outstanding literary contributions. Through its prestigious honours, the Trust celebrates creative excellence in Hindi and regional languages.

Prestigious Awards

Major Literary Awards

◆

Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra Smriti Tathagat Samman (Hindi)

Instituted in memory of Padma Shri Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra, this honour recognises excellence in Hindi literature by writers from non-Hindi-speaking regions (A. Tathagat Sahitya Samman, B. Tathagat Yuva Sahitya Samman).

Genres: Poetry, Short Stories, Novels

Eligibility: Works published between 2020–2025

Award: ₹1,01,000, Shawl, Citation & Memento

Rahul Sankrityayan Lokbhasha Bhojpuri Tathagat Samman

Instituted in memory of Padma Shri Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra, this honour recognises exceptional Hindi literature.

Genres: Poetry, Short Stories, Novels

Eligibility: Works published between 2020–2025

Award: ₹51,000, Shawl, Citation & Memento

Bhagwati Lal Sen Lokbhasha Chhattisgarhi Tathagat Samman

Honouring excellence in Chhattisgarhi language and literature.

Genres: Poetry, Stories, Novels, Plays

Award: ₹51,000, Shawl, Citation & स्मृति चिन्ह

Selection Process

All awards are decided by independent expert committees through a transparent and merit-based evaluation.

Future Vision

Tathagat Trust plans to expand these honours to other regional, folk, and indigenous languages, strengthening India's rich multilingual literary heritage. Tathagat Trust believes that every language carries a legacy and every writer deserves recognition.
